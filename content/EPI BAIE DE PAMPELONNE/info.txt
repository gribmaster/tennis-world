description: Epi began in 1959, when Pampelonne was becoming the summer playground of Saint-Tropez. From Audrey Hepburn to Alain Delon and Brigitte Bardot, the place became part of the Riviera’s golden-age mythology: beach, privacy, music, and beautiful people arriving quietly behind the dunes. Its tennis courts continue that same spirit. Hidden just behind Pampelonne Beach, they feel less like a formal club and more like a private Saint-Tropez ritual: clay courts, bamboo, sea air, and the soft sound of tennis replacing the noise of the beach. A place where the Riviera feels relaxed, discreet, and very 1960s.
