description: "Câteau de la Messardière sits on a hill above Saint-Tropez, between the village, the vineyards of Ramatuelle, and Pampelonne Beach. Originally a 19th-century private château built as a wedding gift, today it feels like a separate world above the busiest part of the Riviera.
Its tennis court is interesting because of the contrast: below, Saint-Tropez is all yachts, parties, and summer noise; above, the court feels quiet and private, set inside a large estate with gardens and warm southern air. Not a tournament legend, but a modern Riviera fantasy: morning tennis before the village wakes up, and the feeling that Saint-Tropez can still be discreet."
